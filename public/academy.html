The Lemba-Kongo Academy is where elite preparation meets unparalleled potential. We develop athletes with the technical brilliance and psychological resilience demanded by the highest levels of global football.

ABOUT

The Lemba-Kongo Academy is an institution devoted to researching, preserving, and expanding the academic and cultural traditions of the Diaspora. We recruit and train successive generations of men to provide for their families and communities while embodying our traditions and spirit.

Our holistic approach to developing young men incorporates three career development pathways simultaneously: (1) Divinity Studies, (2) Academics, (3) Athletics.

Our program is boarding only. Academicians are selected via a series of referrals, camps, and tests. Prospects must have begun the onset of puberty and be 15 years of age or older prior to registering for their residency.*

Funding covers up to eight (8) years of room and board per resident.

DORMS

Each Resident lives in a suburban townhome, owned and maintained by the Academy, in the village next to our training ground. First-Year Residents and younger Academicians will be paired with another Resident of their rank along with an Advisor. After their fifth semester, Academicians are eligible for individual housing. Townhomes are alloted based on the Academician's rank and unit availability.

TRAINING & CURRICULUM

The curriculum is taught on a partial block schedule. Classes are generally taken once every other day. Each Academician has a uniquely individualized curriculum based on their Advisor's evaluations. A given week has at most four (4) days dedicated to the curriculum (2 Blocks), two (2) days dedicated to matches, and one (1) day dedicated to recovery.
